HDdrum

ST's HDdrum is a 'virtual drummer'. It is able to play drum loops in 2/4, 3/4, 4/4, 5/4, 5/8, 6/8, 7/4, 7/8 and 9/8 - something you might search for a very long time...
Meanwhile, the composition and sequence management is coded. The creation of WAV files is next on my todo list.
