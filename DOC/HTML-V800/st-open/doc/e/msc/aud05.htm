st2mo() converts a stereophonic [source] into a monaural [target] file. [target] must have a size of at least one half of [source]. The volume is computed in the mixing process and can't be adjusted. st2mo can handle 8 bit as well as 16 bit WAV files.
[source] must start with a valid WAV header. Its information is required for the conversion process.

DD MyFunction(VOID *source, VOID *target)

 return(rc);

256 kB from the stereophonic [source] are converted to a monaural [target] with a size of 128 kB.
